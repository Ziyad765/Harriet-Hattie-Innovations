About Harriet & Hattie Innovations

A multi-domain engineering powerhouse delivering excellence across renewable energy, water treatment, and industrial automation.

Renewable Energy Division

We specialize in comprehensive solar solutions, from system design and installation to maintenance and monitoring. Our expertise spans on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid systems, along with solar street lights and water pumps.

"Harnessing the power of the sun to create sustainable energy solutions for a brighter tomorrow."

Water & ETP Solutions

Complete effluent treatment plant design, installation, operation, and maintenance services. We handle ETP upgradation, PCB documentation, water and effluent testing, and offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts.

"Protecting our water resources through innovative treatment solutions and sustainable practices."

Industrial Machinery & Automation

Custom machine design and fabrication, production machinery manufacturing, and advanced automation integration using PLC, SCADA, and IoT technologies. We also provide machine refurbishment, maintenance, and R&D support.

"Engineering precision and innovation to drive industrial excellence and operational efficiency."
